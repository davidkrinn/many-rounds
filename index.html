How to Use

The Number System

1 Jab — Quick punch with your lead hand
2 Cross — Power punch with your rear hand
3 Lead Hook — Hook with your lead hand
4 Rear Hook — Hook with your rear hand
5 Lead Uppercut — Uppercut with your lead hand
6 Rear Uppercut — Uppercut with your rear hand

Defensive Moves (Champ Only)

SLIP — Move your head side-to-side to avoid punches
ROLL — Roll your shoulders to deflect incoming punches
BLOCK — Raise your hands to block incoming punches
STEP BACK — Create distance by stepping backward

Setting Up a Match

  1. Tap "Create a Match" to customize your training
  2. Choose your rounds, timing, and difficulty level
  3. Select your preferred voice and music
  4. Tap "Start Match" to begin
  5. Tap "Pause" anytime if you need a break

Tips for Training

  • Start with Beginner if you're new to boxing
  • Use landscape mode if you prop your phone up
  • Wear headphones for best audio experience
  • Move to an open space before starting

About

About Many Rounds

Many Rounds is a free, web-based boxing training app that combines a fully customizable round timer with an intelligent combo engine. Whether you're a beginner learning the basics or an advanced fighter looking for challenging drills, Many Rounds adapts to your level.

Features

  • Customizable rounds, timing, and difficulty levels
  • 8 unique voice profiles to keep training fresh
  • Multiple difficulty levels with defensive moves at the highest level
  • Background music selection (Rock, Metal, Rap)
  • Save your favorite match configurations
  • Works offline — no internet required
  • Installable as a PWA for a native app experience
